PooShooter: Toilet Invaders
Pooshooter: Toilet Invaders is a 2D action comedy game packed with explosions and fun gameplay. Reminiscent of arcade classics like Space Invaders, PooShooter lets you take control of a Bum fighting against vengeful Toilets.  
 
PooShooter: Toilet Invaders offers tons of levels which increase in difficulty, where you can blast more and more Toilets while dodging their projectile Plungers. There are also bonus Golden Toilets that occasionally fly across the top of the screen for extra points.

Sono Hanabira ni Kuchizuke o: Anata to Koibito Tsunagi
Sono Hanabira ni Kuchizuke o: Anata to Koibito Tsunagi is an adult visual novel and the third game in the Sono Hanabira ni Kuchizuke o series of games. The story follows Sawaguchi Mai, who is involved with her classmate Kawamura Reo and who ends up confessing to her.

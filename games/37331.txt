Namco classics volume 1
Namco Classic Collection Volume 1(ナムコクラシックコレクション) is a compilation arcade game that was released by Namco in November 1995. It is a collection of three popular Namco arcade games - Galaga (1981), Xevious (1982) and Mappy (1983). In addition to the original games, there are "Arrangement" versions that are essentially sequels to the original games, with updated graphics and sound. Super Xevious (1984) can be selected from the "Xevious" menu.

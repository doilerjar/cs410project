MDK2: Armageddon
BioWare's hit game, MDK, has a sequel. MDK2 Armageddon gives the players 10 levels, over 20 types of enemies, three playable characters with unique levels and items for each, and deep story. You can play as Kurt Hectic, Doctor Fluke Hawkins and Max - a six-legged dog - and all three characters have different styles of play. Expect humor and wackiness as well as amazing graphics and gameplay. Sit back and get ready for an explosive, action-packed sequel headed towards the PlayStation 2.

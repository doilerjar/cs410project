Devoured Time
Devoured Time is an RPG that takes place in world ravaged by creatures from strange rifts. You choose from a male or female who’s recently arrived from a teleportation platform with no memory of who you are or where you came from. To learn who you are you must first figure out a way to return to where you came from. 
Devoured Time’s content 
•	Main Quest (Short Story) 
•	Side Quest 
•	30 Hidden Collectibles 
•	10 weapons to find and upgrade 
•	Special Mage quest 
Free content updates will follow including Survival mode and Conquest mode.
